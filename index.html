Our Story

Bringing together emerging talent and established creative forerunners

Jefferson House is an open-air courtyard venue designed to bring together local restaurants, curated retail, a neighborhood bar, live music, and the people who make the West Valley creative.

Built on the proven model of The Churchill in downtown Phoenix, we know what it takes to create spaces where small businesses can come together and thrive. This is not a speculative concept. It is a model that already works.

In partnership with The Churchill · Banquet · City of Peoria
