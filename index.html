Jefferson House is a courtyard venue in Peoria's Arts District bringing together local food, independent retail, live entertainment, and community events in one open-air space. We're currently under construction and actively looking for tenants, event partners, and people who want to be part of something new on the west side.

A Place for the
West Side.

Jefferson House is a small group of people who believe Peoria's Arts District deserves a gathering place built around local business, live arts, and community. We're working with Banquet and the City of Peoria to make it happen.

Right now we're in the build phase, actively looking for independent food and retail operators who want to be part of it from the start. If this sounds like your kind of thing, or if you just want to follow along, we're happy to talk.
